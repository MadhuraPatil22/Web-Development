Dr. D Y Patil College of Engineering

About Us

Dr. D Y Patil College of Engineering and Technology, Kasaba Bawada, Kolhapur is a self-financed, Autonomous Institute established in the year 1984 under the visionary leadership of Padma Shree Dr. D.Y. Patil, Former Governor of Tripura, Bihar and West Bengal.
UGC, New Delhi and Shivaji University, Kolhapur have conferred Autonomous Status to the institute in the year 2020.
